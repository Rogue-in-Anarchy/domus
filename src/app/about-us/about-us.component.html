About us

Domus Perfectus Nigeria Limit ed (DP) is building finishes firm which provides interior design services, landscaping services and the supply of sophisticated electrical fixtures accessories, ETC. It is a home based company with its main office is in Abuja. We provide interior design and landscaping services for discerning, quality-conscious clients that seek assistance in their design choices for their primary residences, vacation homes and businesses. This experience offers personal attention through the design process and also provides design resources and products to its clients through special purchases of lightning fixtures, furniture, fabric and accessories. The total experience is provided in a way to Inform, Inspire and Assist clients through the process of transforming their home or business environment to become a unique and personalized expression of themselves and adding to their enjoyment of that interior spaces.
